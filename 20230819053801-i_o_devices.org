#+title: I/O Devices
#+filetags: :OS:

These include our *monitors*, *keyboards*, *mice*, *hard* *disk* *drives *(HDD)*, *speakers*, *Bluetooth* *headsets*, *webcams* and *network* *adapters*.

These I/O devices are all managed by our [[id:cba22c0a-759f-4b3a-9ee4-25663f8eb434][Kernel]], the [[id:cba22c0a-759f-4b3a-9ee4-25663f8eb434][Kernel]] needs to be able to load up drivers that are used so that we can recognize and speak to these different types of hardware.
