#+title: Complementary Metal-Oxide Semiconductor (CMOS)

Stores basic data about booting your computer, like the date, time, and how you want it to start up.

You can change these settings by booting into =CMOS= or [[id:de18822a-c4d8-4094-90d6-359ec6b87e83][BIOS]] settings menu.
