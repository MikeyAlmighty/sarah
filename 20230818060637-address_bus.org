#+title: Address Bus

Connects the =CPU= to the [[id:48d810ed-552a-460e-b9d7-c93128d10a1c][MCC]] and sends over the *location* (Address) of the data but not the data itself, then the [[id:48d810ed-552a-460e-b9d7-c93128d10a1c][MCC]] takes the address and looks for the data at said address, then the data is sent over using the [[id:33c77b2f-9280-4216-9aa9-78870c9f23c9][External Data Bus (EDB)]].
