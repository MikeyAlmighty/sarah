#+title: Pretty Good Privacy (PGP)

Developed by [[id:c18638e4-256b-470f-b9bd-367454767ffc][Phil Zimmermann]] in *1991*

[[id:3389ae98-fd3b-4efc-88d7-e9befaae940c][Encryption]] application that allows *authentication* of data, along with *privacy* from third parties, relying upon [[id:9e9c3969-d173-4137-8fab-e671aab2f87b][Asymmetric Encryption]] to achieve this.
