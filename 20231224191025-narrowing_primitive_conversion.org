#+title: Narrowing Primitive Conversion

Converting from a wider primitive type to a narrower primitive type

It can result in a *loss* of ~magnitude~ information, and possibly in a loss of ~precision~ as well.

Usually require a [[id:3ef23225-ae37-428e-8ad3-5d7728f59478][cast]].
