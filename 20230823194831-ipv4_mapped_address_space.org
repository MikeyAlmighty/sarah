#+title: IPv4 Mapped Address Space

Any [[id:fe76a7c6-0798-459c-a237-47262c7cfa5b][IPv6 address]] that begins with *80* zeros and followed by *16* ones

The remaining 32 bits of the IPv6 address is just the same 32 bits of the IPv4 address it's meant to represent.
