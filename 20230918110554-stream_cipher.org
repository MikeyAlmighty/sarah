#+title: Stream Cipher

Takes a stream of input and encrypts the stream *one* =character/digit= at a time outputting *one* encrypted =character/digit= at a time.

Faster to implement than [[id:2acddcbe-0490-4b4b-9c80-707dc3592d9c][block Ciphers]] albeit *Less secure*
