#+title: GROUP BY
#+property: header-args :tangle code/sql/group_by.sql

The ~GROUP BY~ clause is used in [[id:e86502bc-990e-4ac3-86e9-4b06f475b5e9][SQL]] syntax to group *rows* in a table based on given *columns* into *summary rows*, also known as *subgroups*.

Used with [[id:267d0701-d287-4fd7-b472-4594920cc5bf][Aggregate functions]]

#+BEGIN_SRC sql
SELECT Department
FROM orders
GROUP BY Department;
#+END_SRC
