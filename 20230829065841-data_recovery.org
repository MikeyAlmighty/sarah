#+title: Data Recovery

Process of trying to *restore* data after an unexpected event that results in =data loss= or =corruption=


When an unexpected event occurs, your main objective is to =resume normal operations= as soon as possible while minimizing the disruption to business functions.

Best way to be prepared for a *data loss* event is to have a [[id:4e9b5bed-a4b9-4c0e-b8b2-68a06cc69b19][Disaster plan]] and [[id:9cb59965-84a7-48d2-9ac5-dc2912899e05][Data Restoration Procedures]] in place.


* Storage Options
** Local storage
| Pros                      | Cons                                |
|---------------------------+-------------------------------------|
| Data is physically nearby | Data loss due to damage at location |
| Low bandwidth needs       |                                     |

** Off-site storage
| Pros                                | Cons                          |
|-------------------------------------+-------------------------------|
| Data is safer in multiple locations | Needs security and encryption |
|                                     | Large amounts of bandwidth    |
