#+title: Unix
#+filetags: :OS:

Started in *1969*

A family of multitasking, multi-user computer [[id:1dcff204-74cf-445d-9357-5c01ca3d2a03][Operating Systems]] that derive from the original =AT&T= Unix, whose development started at the [[id:55e2923f-2df5-4586-843e-4abff57bad32][Bell Labs research center]].

By [[id:2f862a56-fd11-431b-b8d1-812aafcacd7f][Ken Thompson]], [[id:7994c754-f128-411a-94b2-11b59350efa0][Dennis Ritchie]], and others.
