#+title: LEFT JOIN

The =LEFT JOIN= returns all records from the *left* table , and the matching records from the *right* table.

#+BEGIN_SRC sql
SELECT column_name(s)
FROM table1
LEFT JOIN table2
ON table1.column_name = table2.column_name;
#+END_SRC
