#+title: USB Connectors

| 2.0                     | 3.0                  | 3.1                    |
|-------------------------+----------------------+------------------------|
| Black Port              | Blue Port            | Teal Port              |
| 480 Mbps Transfer speed | 5Gbps Transfer speed | 10 Gbps transfer speed |


* Micro USB
Small USB port found on many non-Apple cellphones, tablets, and other portable devices.

* USB-C
Newest reversible connector with either end having the same build.

USB-C cables replace traditional USB connectors since they can carry significantly more power and transfer data at *20 Gbps*.

* USB4
uses Thunderbolt 3 protocol and USB-C cables to transfer data at speeds of *40 Gbps* and provide power as well.

* Lightning Port
Exclusive to Apple that is similar to USB-C.

It is used for *charging* and *connecting* devices to computers, external monitors, cameras and other peripherals.
