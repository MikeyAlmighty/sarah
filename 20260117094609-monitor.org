#+title: Monitor

* Monitor

The ~monitor~ *displays* information (text and graphics).
[[id:a35bf616-c4fe-4669-ac7b-a58d706644e1][Screen Resolution]] and [[id:7525f5a2-56f8-4a1e-a4dc-92cdb24e92ad][Dot Pitch]] determine the *quality* of the display.

[[id:a35bf616-c4fe-4669-ac7b-a58d706644e1][Screen Resolution]]


A common resolution for a 17-inch screen, for example, is 1,024 pixels wide and 768 pixels high.

The resolution can be set manually. The higher the resolution, the sharper and clearer the image is.
