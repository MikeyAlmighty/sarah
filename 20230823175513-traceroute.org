#+title: Traceroute

Lets you discover the paths between two nodes, and gives you information about each hop along the way.

On Linux and macOS, traceroute sends [[id:e71c58f9-1229-4c46-abe6-a963ff4652f7][UDP]] packets to very high port numbers.
