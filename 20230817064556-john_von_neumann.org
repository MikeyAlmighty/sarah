#+title: John von Neumann

* John von Neumann

28 December 1903 - 8 February 1957

John von Neumann was a Hungarian-American mathematician, physicist, computer scientist, engineer and polymath.

Scientific genius who pioneered the modern computer, [[id:eb881dc5-6b9f-426b-82f6-afc9c85c9128][game theory]], nuclear deterrence, and more.
