#+title: Disaster plan

Collection of documented procedures and plans on how to react and handle a disaster scenario from the operational perspective.

Disaster plans should involve making regular backups of any and all critical data that's necessary for ongoing business processes.

This includes things like:
- customer data
- System databases
- System conflicts
- Financial data.
