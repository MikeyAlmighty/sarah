#+title: Port

*16-bit* number used to direct traffic to specific services running on a networked computer.

(=65535= different possible port ids)

Ports are split up by the [[id:241b71d4-8227-4d41-9628-c1dd70b13dcd][IANA]] into three categories:

* System Ports
ports =1-1023=

* User Ports
ports =1024-49151=

* Ephemeral (Dynamic) Ports
ports =59152-65535=
