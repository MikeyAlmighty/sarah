#+title: Input/Output Devices
* Input and Output Devices
~Input~ and ~output~ devices let the user *communicate* with the computer.
The most common ~input~ devices are the [[id:f8593050-9682-4faa-9d74-681554e39d47][Keyboard]] and *[[id:44f09c52-c6e6-459f-b2f3-9ce9177a5d56][Mouse]]*.
The most common ~output~ devices are [[id:e1f85563-c1a7-400d-ac23-c3b32249a3a0][Monitors]] and *printers*.
