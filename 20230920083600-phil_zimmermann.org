#+title: Phil Zimmermann

12 February 1954 (age 69 years)

Zimmermann was an anti-nuclear activist and political activism drove his development of the [[id:3ba86bcc-9825-4b5a-b420-ddb19fba3c05][PGP]] encryption software to facilitate secure communications for other activists.
