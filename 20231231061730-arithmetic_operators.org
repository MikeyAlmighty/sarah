* Arithmetic Operators
A [[id:9bcf2aef-060a-4011-98b5-cb949a9f7d1a][Binary Operator]] that operate on [[id:2483364b-73d3-4169-92b9-1fea473a9b01][numeric values]]

#+ATTR_ORG: :width 700
[[./assets/arithmetic_operators.png]]

Applied to *all* [[id:46582863-7f97-448d-bbc7-f7da4c1e7574][Primitive Types]] *except* for ~Boolean~

Only the addition operators ~+~ and ~+=~ may be applied to *String* values, which results in =String concatenation=.
