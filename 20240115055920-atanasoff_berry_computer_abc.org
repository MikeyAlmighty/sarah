#+title: Atanasoff Berry Computer (ABC)

* Atanasoff Berry Computer (ABC)

The first special-purpose computer that encoded information electrically was invented by [[id:9fc30a03-cf58-4e76-bc5f-f7efe9765ae5][John Vincent Atanasoff]] and his assistant [[id:72297bf9-0042-4499-90c3-4b79d9f046d5][Clifford Berry]] in =1939=.
Was specifically designed to *solve* a ~system of linear equations~.
