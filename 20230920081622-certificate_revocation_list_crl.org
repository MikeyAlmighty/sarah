#+title: Certificate Revocation List (CRL)

List of [[id:dc321a18-5fdd-49c8-8969-0a4752dcb1dd][Digital Certificates]] that have been *revoked* by the issuing [[id:9de3d7ca-682d-44bf-8f69-ac6294632ef0][Certificate Authority (CA)]] *before* their actual or assigned expiration date.
