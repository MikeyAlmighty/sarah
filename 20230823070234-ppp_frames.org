#+title: PPP Frames

* Flag
*1 Byte*
A single byte and lets the receiver know this is the beginning of the frame. Depending on the encapsulation, there may or may not be a start flag or an end flag.

* Address
*1 Byte*
Contains the broadcast address.

* Control
*1 Byte*
A single byte required for various purposes but also allows a connectionless data link.

* Protocol
*1 - 3 Bytes*
Varies from one to three bytes which identify the network protocol of the datagram.

* Data
*limit of 1500 bytes per frame*
Where the information you need to transmit is stored

* Frame check sequence (FCS)
*2 or 4 bytes*
Used to verify data is intact upon receipt at the endpoint.
