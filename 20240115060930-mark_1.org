#+title: Mark 1

* Mark 1

In the =1930s=, the US Navy and IBM sponsored a project at Harvard University under the direction of [[id:0a494cc7-3d7d-47bd-b6c2-c045ddbdfd99][Howard Aiken]] to build a huge computer called ~Mark I~.

This computer used both *electrical* and *mechanical* components.
