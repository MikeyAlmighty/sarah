#+title: Media Access Control Address

*48-bit* number normally represented by 6 groups of two hexadecimal numbers.

A =Media Access Control= Address is a  12-character alphanumeric unique identifier assigned to a [[id:553e78d5-2767-4a53-827e-0386e4e6db56][NIC]] for use as a network address in communications within a network segment.

=MAC addresses= are generally *permanent* and *hard-coded* onto a device.


First three Octets of a MAC Address: Organizationally Unique Identifier (OUI)
