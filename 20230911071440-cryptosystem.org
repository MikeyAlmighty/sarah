#+title: Cryptosystem

A cryptosystem or a collection of algorithms for key generation and [[id:3389ae98-fd3b-4efc-88d7-e9befaae940c][Encryption]] and [[id:e35c79e8-db3e-4c13-9ad3-1a8f4387fa60][Decryption]] operations that comprise a cryptographic service should remain secure, *even if everything about the system is known* =except= for the *key*.
