#+title: Spear Phising Attack


Variation of [[id:c19cee33-b4a3-418d-b91d-407e981690af][Phising]]

Specifically targets individual or group.

The fake emails may contain some personal information like your name or the names of friends or family, so they seem more trustworthy.
