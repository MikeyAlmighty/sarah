#+title: Signal


A signal is a way to tell a [[id:2bc74ef5-d9b1-4286-ab74-bc061c6a1704][Process]] that something's just happened.

i.e.
- =SIGINT= Interrupt Signal
- =SIGKILL= Kill immediately Signal
- =SIGTSTP= Suspend process
- =SIGCONT= Continue suspended process
