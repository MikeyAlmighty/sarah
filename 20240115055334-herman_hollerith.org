#+title: Herman Hollerith

* Herman Hollerith

29 February 1860 - 17 November 1929

A German-American statistician, inventor, and businessman.

In =1890= designed and built a ~programmer machine~ that could automatically *read*, *tally*, and *sort* ~data~ stored on /punched cards/.
