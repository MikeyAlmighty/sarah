#+title: Ethernet Frame

An Ethernet frame is a highly structured collection of information presented in a specific order.

Almost all sections of an =Ethernet frame= are mandatory, and most of them have a fixed size.

#+ATTR_ORG: :width 900
[[./assets/EthernetFrame.png]]


* 1. [[id:37a5b76a-684c-4e08-aece-823a980b6798][Preamble]] (7 + 1 Byte)
* 2. [[id:91c04786-4639-48b5-aa47-7f409a0f777d][Destination Address]] (6 Bytes)
* 3. [[id:7c10dafa-8f93-455c-a49f-a7ad391971ec][Source Address]] (6 Bytes)
* 4. [[id:c9f3f2fa-cf2b-401e-a25e-56f334868ea3][Ether Type]] (2 Bytes)
* 4. (Alternatively) [[id:6324e476-df5c-4919-b766-c0b50e480a20][VLAN Header]]  (4 Btytes)
* 5. [[id:a078af54-3a0d-4144-8878-025f18b61941][Payload]]
* 6. [[id:38851cb0-bf78-43b8-b28e-17bcbb7d6555][FCS]]
