#+title: Ken Thompson

4 Feb 1943 -
=Kenneth Lane Thompson=

Thompson worked at [[id:55e2923f-2df5-4586-843e-4abff57bad32][Bell Labs]] for most of his career where he designed and implemented the original [[id:aa65acf3-8a3f-4ce2-918f-975ff273faa7][Unix]] OS.

Also invented the [[id:5fb77ae2-7fc8-4f23-904b-8663892017dd][B Programming Language]] the direct predecessor to [[id:c3431378-98ee-42e8-b7b8-8732b5d14c6a][C Programming Language]].
Since 2006 has worked at Google, where he co-developed the [[id:a87c6bc9-87e6-4aba-a656-c4e34bebd037][Go lang]]
