#+title: USB Flash Drives

* USB Flash Drives
[[id:a29de489-512f-46c8-97dc-dc3519dd4893][USB Connectors]] allow the user to attach many kinds of peripheral devices to the computer.

An ~USB~ flash drive is a device for *storing* and *transporting* data.
~USB~ flash drives are currently available with up to *256* *GB* storage capacity.
