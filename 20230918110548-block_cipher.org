#+title: Block Cipher

The [[id:ff735327-4e77-43b6-8755-50ceccc7f9a0][cipher]] takes data in, places it into a *block of data (bucket)* that's fixed in size, then encodes that entire block as *one* unit.

More secure than [[id:1559390c-b0ff-4a78-9c66-7ff70979e190][stream Ciphers]]
