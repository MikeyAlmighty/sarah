#+title: Port Forwarding

Technique where specific *destination* ports can be configured to always be delivered to *specific* nodes.
This technique allows for complete [[id:f909d926-7b90-4b28-984f-e0fe27173709][IP Masquerading]] while still having services that can respond to incoming traffic.
