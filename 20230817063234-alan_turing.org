#+title: Alan Turing

* Alan Turing

23 June 1912 - 7 June 1954

While completing his degrees, he developed the concept of the [[id:5a277bbe-5ff6-464c-b8d0-eb858f7179e3][Turing Machine]].

During the Second World War, Turing developed the [[id:d0fa831e-3a0a-4e0c-8bc4-0397ee4aec2a][Turing-Welchman Bombe]].

After the war, Turing's [[id:782b9856-f518-465a-aca4-7f3877c73809][Turing Test (Imitation Game)]] was created.
