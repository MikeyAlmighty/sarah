#+title: Linus Torvalds

28 December 1969 -

Finnish software engineer who is the creator and lead developer of the [[id:5c6ee0bf-4232-4ea7-8117-cfd39937a099][Linux]] [[id:cba22c0a-759f-4b3a-9ee4-25663f8eb434][Kernel]], used by Linux distributions like Debian, Arch and Android.

He also created the distributed Version Control System [[id:3325238a-a3d7-42b2-b999-52fb6716c9f5][Git]].
