#+title: Swap Space (Swap File)

=Swap Space= helps the computer's  [[id:1dcff204-74cf-445d-9357-5c01ca3d2a03][OS]] in pretending that it has more RAM than it actually has.

It is also called a swap file.

This interchange of data between [[id:f684616d-edd4-4635-92d0-c8c94cc921e4][Virtual Memory]] and real memory([[id:73ee7657-201d-4041-acfd-c05bdfaeb118][RAM]]) is called =swapping=.
