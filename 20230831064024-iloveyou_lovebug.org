#+title: ILoveYou (lovebug)

On and after 5 May 2000

[[id:df4f5fcd-dbed-465a-a309-01526b71a7c4][Worm]] that spread to over ten million of [[id:45bb23df-e29d-46dc-b704-f17cf26af1d7][Windows]] machines.

The worm was spread via [[id:3fb70617-0f04-4adf-8198-a3ee461c4226][email]].

Someone would email a message with the subject line of "I love you" and an attachment that was actually the worm disguised as a love letter text file.

Caused billions of Dollars in damage
