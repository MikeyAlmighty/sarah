#+title: Broadcast

In [[id:514c3f0b-c3ec-46f1-aa28-c2a4874a6a7b][ethernet]], =broadcast= is sent to *every* single device on a LAN.

This is accomplished by using a special *destination* known as a [[id:a22998c6-f45c-4ddb-88cc-ffc9aff917f4][Broadcast Address]].
