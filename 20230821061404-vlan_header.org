#+title: VLAN Header

Itself is what's called a [[id:1a4d6240-2076-4af8-b7fb-c3f7fb0f616e][VLAN]] frame.

If a [[id:1a4d6240-2076-4af8-b7fb-c3f7fb0f616e][VLAN]] header is present, the [[id:c9f3f2fa-cf2b-401e-a25e-56f334868ea3][Ether Type]] field follows it.
