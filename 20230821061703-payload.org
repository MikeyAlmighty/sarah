#+title: Payload

Data payload of an [[id:73b06fc5-e631-40ad-828f-741dd84bd15c][Ethernet Frame]].

A payload in networking terms is the =actual data= being transported
(Everything that is *not* a header)

The data payload of a traditional Ethernet frame can be anywhere from *46-1500* Bytes long.
