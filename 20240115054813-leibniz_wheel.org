#+title: Leibniz Wheel

* Leibniz Wheel

=Late 17th Century=

More sophisticated (than [[id:5f1a7be8-3673-4ead-b491-b1fd65a064c8][Pascaline]]) mechanical calculator that could do ~multiplication~ and ~division~ as well as ~addition~ and ~subtraction~.
Invented by [[id:c4a2a5c0-1285-403c-b3ca-f8dbce732e8a][Gottfried Leibniz]].
