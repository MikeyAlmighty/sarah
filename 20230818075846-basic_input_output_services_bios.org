#+title: Basic Input Output Services (BIOS)

The =BIOS= is software that helps initialize the hardware in our computer and gets our operating system up and running.

Stored in [[id:e775353a-5ad0-4422-bbd5-f5fee16d7998][ROM]] chip on motherboard.

In today's system there's another player for =BIOS= called [[id:0e0f66f9-1c8b-4604-b6a9-2d54d8509511][UEFI]].

There's a special chip on our motherboard called the [[id:91630f23-3932-4e0e-8a66-38f871a77276][CMOS]] chip.
