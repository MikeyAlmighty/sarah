#+title: Phising Attack

A type of [[id:99c92f26-62e7-4d66-a978-87781d57e914][Social Engineering]] attack

=Phishing= usually occurs when a malicious email is sent to a victim disguised as something legitimate.

One common phishing attack is an [[id:3fb70617-0f04-4adf-8198-a3ee461c4226][email]] saying your bank account has been compromised.

It then gives you a link to click on to reset your password.

When you go to the link, it looks like your bank's website, but it's actually a fake website so your tricked into entering your current password and credentials in order to reset your current password.
