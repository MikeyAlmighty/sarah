Family of open-source [[id:aa65acf3-8a3f-4ce2-918f-975ff273faa7][Unix]]-like operating systems based on the Linux kernel:
An [[id:1dcff204-74cf-445d-9357-5c01ca3d2a03][OS]] [[id:cba22c0a-759f-4b3a-9ee4-25663f8eb434][Kernel]] first released on *September 17 1991*, by [[id:4d6f88bb-2555-48b3-a949-062d4e38b8a9][Linus Torvalds]].


* Permission Levels
=rwx=
4 for R (Read)
2 for W (Write)
1 for X (Execute)

=rwxrwxrwx= First Triplet: User, second Triplet: Group, Third Triplet: Other Users
