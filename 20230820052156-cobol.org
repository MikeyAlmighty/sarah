#+title: COBOL

First release *1959*

=COBOL= is a compiled English-like computer [[id:377878e6-ec53-439b-bd7d-50af9a365696][Programming Language]] designed for business use.

It is an imperative, procedural and, since 2002, object-oriented language.

COBOL is primary use:
- Business
- Finance
- Administrative systems for companies and governments
