#+title: Naming Conventions

* Naming Conventions

Set of standardised *rules* and *guidelines* when naming =variables=, =methods=, =classes=, =constants=, and =other= =elements= in their code.

These conventions ensure *consistency*, *readability*, and *maintainability* code.

- Improves readability
- Enhances code maintainability
- Promotes industry standards
- Avoids confusion
- Simplifies debugging

** Common Java naming conventions

*** Classes
- =PascalCase=
- *nouns*
- ~class AnimalInfo {}~

*** Methods
- =camelCase=
- *verbs*
- ~void calculateTotalPrice() {}~

*** Variables
- =camelCase=
- *Meaningful*
- ~int totalNumber = 5;~

*** Constants
- =UPPERCASE_WITH_UNDERSCORES=
- ~final double INTEREST_RATE = 13.2978;~

*** Packages
- =lowercase=
- ~package com.example.myapp;~
