#+title: RIGHT JOIN

The =RIGHT JOIN= returns all records from the *right* table , and the matching records from the *left* table.

#+BEGIN_SRC sql
SELECT column_name(s)
FROM table1
RIGHT JOIN table2
ON table1.column_name = table2.column_name;
#+END_SRC
