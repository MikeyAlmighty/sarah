#+title: Evil Twin Attack

Similar to [[id:2b080cf1-ae5f-42dd-8ed9-535d1d4ef5b8][Rouge AP Attack]]

The premise of an =Evil twin= attack is for you to connect to a network that is identical to yours.

This identical network is our networks =Evil twin-= and is *controlled* by our *attacker*.
