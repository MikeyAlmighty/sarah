#+title: WannaCry

May 2017

The [[id:28bfd4d2-9ece-4616-9f0a-666469384dbd][malware]] took advantage of a [[id:66f1b4f0-152a-4006-803f-1a78423ea6ce][vulnerability]] in older [[id:45bb23df-e29d-46dc-b704-f17cf26af1d7][Windows]] systems, infecting hundreds of thousands of machines across the world.

Most notably, the attacks shutdown the systems for the =National Health Services in England=, causing a health-related crisis.

The WannaCry ransomware attack devastated systems around the world.
