#+title: Internet Control Message Protocl (ICMP)

=ICMP= mainly used by a [[id:88dd44ee-b7ff-4a29-9048-10709d05bb8e][router]] or remote host to communicate why transmission has failed back to the origin of the transmission.

[[id:958904bc-6a62-45e5-a508-9f996ca87212][Network Layer]] protocl used by network devices to diagnose network communication issues.

Mainly used to determine whether or not data is reaching its intended destination in a timely manner.

ICMP is crucial for error reporting and testing, but it can also be used in distributed denial-of-service (DDoS) attacks.
