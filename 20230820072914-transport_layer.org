#+title: 3. Transport Layer

Allows traffic to be directed to specific network *[[id:8580f7d1-7d1b-4922-9e73-7a7e2c9318bd][applications]]*.

While the [[id:958904bc-6a62-45e5-a508-9f996ca87212][Network Layer]] delivers data between two individual nodes, the =transport layer= sorts out which client and server programs are supposed to get that data.
