#+title: Botnets

Designed to utilize the power of the internet-connected machines to perform some distributed function

Because of [[id:28bfd4d2-9ece-4616-9f0a-666469384dbd][malware's]] potential ability to communicate with other bad actors, you should quarantine the infected device ([[id:c4704640-3daa-44f1-8586-109aa88fb011][Anti-Malware protection]]).
