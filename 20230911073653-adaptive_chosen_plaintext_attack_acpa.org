#+title: Adaptive Chosen-Plaintext Attack (ACPA)

Similar to [[id:453c694f-ed6a-44e6-8277-cdfdde743c23][CPA]].

Unlike a [[id:453c694f-ed6a-44e6-8277-cdfdde743c23][CPA]], it can use smaller lines of =plaintext= to receive its encrypted =ciphertext= and then crack the encryption code using the =ciphertext=.
